Always in the lead: the new GLE Coupé

Design

01 / Exterior

The gently sloping windshield gives the vehicle a high aerodynamic performance and matches better with the sloping roofline, thus contributing to the feeling of harmonious elegance of the coupé. The diamond-style radiator grille with horizontal louvres speaks of its sportiness. The muscular design gives the GLE Coupé a look that speaks of power and readiness to jump. In combination with the narrow and elongated taillights, the muscles accentuate the width of the rear end. Fitted flush with the wheel arches, rims (ranging from 19 to 22 inches) accentuate the SUV character of this vehicle.

02 / Interior

Get behind the wheel and ... rest. In the new GLE Coupé, you can enjoy massage programs accompanied by LED interior lighting in 64 colors. Or use the ENERGIZING function, which integrates the various comfort systems in the car into a single network, uses light and music to create mood, and offers various massage modes for a wide variety of comfort and wellness programs.
